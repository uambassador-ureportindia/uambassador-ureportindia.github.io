Who are U-Ambassadors?

U-Report, UNICEF's digital youth engagement platform, boasts 2.8 million U-Reporters in India, with a whopping 90% aged 15-30.

Now, we're forging a fresh wave of leaders – the U-Ambassadors.

Our mission is to amplify youth voices through U-Report, represent us at events, and extend our reach to empower Indian youth and communities. Join the movement!

Recruitment Process.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. You have to be between ages 16 to 25 years.

  2. You should have completed at least 3 Polls.

  3. You should have completed at least 8 funDoo Tasks.

  4. You should have done at least 5 Referrals.

Incentives

  1. Certificate from UNICEF.

  2. Learning Essential Job Skills.

  3. Chance to Represent U-Report India nationally.

  4. Rewards for Good Performance.

  5. Distinct Recognitions for Exceptional Performance.

Roles and Responsibilities

As an U-Ambassador, you will be committed to:

  1. Connect more of your friends, families, and

    peers with U-Report India.

  2. Sharing U-Report activities in your networks, both online and offline.

  3. Disseminating U-Report poll results to raise awareness and drive change.

  4. Contributing to U-Report in your area of expertise, be it communication, design, outreach, and more.

  5. Actively participating in online meetings.

  6. Devoting a minimum of 1 hour daily or 5 hours weekly to program-related tasks.
